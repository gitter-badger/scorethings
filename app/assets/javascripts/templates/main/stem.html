STEM (science, technology, engineering and mathematics)

Check out these cool STEM programs in Seattle. Please follow them on Twitter and visit their websites for info on how to donate.

Technology Access Foundation (TAF)

Website: http://techaccess.org
Follow @stembytaf
  • It helps kids in communities of color and those with low incomes to learn STEM skills.
  • It has an academy with a project-based curriculum in a wide variety of STEM subjects.
  • I attended their program in high school, and it got me interested in a career in tech.

Ada Developers Academy (Ada)

Website: http://adadevelopersacademy.org
Follow @adaacademy
  • An intensive, year long, software developer training school for women.
  • Helps support students as they take time to learn new tech skills.
  • Challenging coursework that includes cutting edge technology and techniques.
