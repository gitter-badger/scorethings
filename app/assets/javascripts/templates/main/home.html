Welcome to scorethings

scorethings is a webapp I'm starting to help users "score things", or, give a thing a score.

What's a thing? Any Twitter user, hashtag, or tweet.

What's a score? A score is a number of points (0-100) that YOU give that thing in a category.

What's a category? It's something to help you easily describe the score you're giving a thing.

scorethings provides you with 10 different categories to use when scoring a thing.

You can give a thing as many scores as you want. You can also share that score with your friends. Check out some scores that I made.
