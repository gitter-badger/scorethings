What's a score?

A score is a number of points (0-100) that YOU give a thing.

What's a thing?

Any Twitter account, tweet or YouTube video that you have access to. Also any hashtag you want.

Why do you keep using the words "score" and "thing"?

scorethings provides you with 11 different categories to help you describe your score........of a thing.

Try it out.

If you want to quit, you can easily delete all of your scorethings scores.
