Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What's a score?

A score is a number of points (0-100) that YOU give a thing. I'm thinking it will be pretty subjective. Like, one person's 80 could be inflated. The idea is to have a number describe how much a thing can be described by a score category.

Yeah, that sounded weird. Okay, let's say you give Patton Oswalt's twitter account @pattonoswalt, a Funny score of 90, that would mean you think the twitter account @pattonoswalt is pretty funny. Then, if you give Matt Oswalt, @puddinstrip, a Funny score of 95, you would be saying you realize @puddinstrip is funnier.


What's a score category?

A score category is an adjective that describes a thing.

scorethings provides you with 12 different categories to help you describe your score........of a thing.
